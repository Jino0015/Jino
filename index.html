The Secret is in the Smoke

At The Golden Lechon, we believe lechon is the undisputed king of the Filipino feast. Our pigs are organic-fed and stuffed with a proprietary blend of lemongrass, scallions, and local spices before being slow-roasted over charcoal for six hours. The result? A glass-like skin and meat so tender it falls off the bone.

The Skin: Our "Glass-Skin" technique ensures a loud crunch in every single bite.
The Spice: We use native bird's eye chilies for a heat that builds beautifully.
Lechon Fact: The word "Lechon" comes from the Spanish word for suckling pig.
Pairing: Best enjoyed with hot puso (hanging rice) and a vinegar-soy dip.
